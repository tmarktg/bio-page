Why are you taking this course and what do you hope to get out of this course?

I wanted to be able to build a portfolio to start applying for web developer jobs. Also, due to my low interaction with web developers because of online classes I've been taking at Mesa College, I want to gauge my skills as a developer to get an understanding of where my skill is compared to a group of students who are trying to become web developers. Further, I want to be able to meet a group of people to discuss their goals, and struggles and relate to them as I grow as a web developer. I hope to gain the necessary knowledge to build a protfolio to be able to get into a company and gain experience as a web developer. My goal for this course is to be able to present 5 projects that would be practical to showcase my skills for front-end web development.

What web design or web page design/development or any web creation tools, programming languages, digital imaging, print, drawing programs, or graphic creation experience do you have?

I have been self-learning web development for close to a year. I have learnt HTML, CSS, and JS, and have some sense of image creation through canva. I also played around with some frameworks such as bootstrap, tailwindcss, and jQuery. I am also aware of photoshop and have seen some tutorials on how to use it. I haven't made any great strides in learning material, probably because I've been focusing too much on learning rather than doing. I find myself knowing how to do things but only after there are a series of steps for me to follow in order to do it.

I enjoy boxing, and playing chess in my free time.
